About Djo

Full name: Joseph David Keery Born: April 24, 1992 He's an American actor and musician. As an actor, he's best known for playing Steve Harrington in Stranger Things. Under the musical alias Djo (pronounced like “Joe”), he releases solo music.

Before going solo, Joe was involved with the psychedelic rock band Post Animal in Chicago. He parted ways with the band around 2019 to focus more on his own music. Music Feeds With Djo, he aims for a sound blending psychedelia, synth-pop, indie rock, and retro influences. Joe once said he chose a stage name so that the music could exist somewhat apart from his acting identity.
